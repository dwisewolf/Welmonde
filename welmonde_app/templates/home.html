    F.A.Q

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • How frequently is a health checkup required?

      Depending on your risk level, you will need to be tested Annually or once every three years.

    • Why is an Annual Health Checkup important?

      An Annual Health Checkup can help find the potential issues before they become a problem. By getting the correct health services, screenings and treatment you are taking important steps towards living a longer, healthier life.

    • How is that Health Checkup Conducted?

      Our team will carry all the assessments and a dedicated health check team will be assigned to escort you through the stages of the health checkup. You will be initially subjected to the definitive investigations depending upon the package. When the results are examined by a qualified doctor, suitable medical advice is given & necessary steps to improve your health.

    • Why does an employee need a health checkup?

      The employee requires health checkup if any problem that may pose a significant impairment in doing a job which may put your coworkers or your work at risk.

    • How are the samples carried to the lab at the time of Onsite camp?

      Once the sample is taken it will be sent to the testing laboratory. Sometimes it has to reach the lab within a certain time frame to ensure it is valid for testing.

    • Difference between Welmonde services and other services?

      Welmonde provides quality service that is affordable and accessible to all, with discounted offers.

    • Can I go for the medical checkups in the evening?

      If you are going for a full body health checkup then it is not possible, as the sample has to be collected early morning with 12hrs of fasting.

    • How long will the entire health checkup process take?

      It takes several minutes to complete some paperwork and about one hour to conduct the screening, depending on the number of tests, your vascular anatomy and your body type.

    • Can my dependents avail the health check?

      Yes, you can purchase certain packages for your dependents to avail the services.

    • Whom should I contact in case of any query during the checkup?

      You can reach Welmonde customer care for end to end support.
